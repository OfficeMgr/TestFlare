CDS083

Determine Day of Week Given Any Date

 Last Revised: 11/29/97

CDS083 can be called to return the day of the week given any date.

This program was needed especially on systems before the BBx DATE() function was available, but can still be used.

Calling Format:

CALL "CDS083", Z1$, Z2$

CDS083
Z1$ Any date passed in the format of "YYMMDD" or "MM/DD/YY"
Z2$ Will be returned as a day of the week (ex. "WEDNESDAY")
